Schooling History

Before College

Ever since I was a little kid, I have always attended schools in Marion County. From kindergarten to 5th grade, I went to Lakeside Elementary school. From there, I went to Stonybrook Middle School for a year. This was around the time when the school district mapping changed for whatever reason and the next year I got sent to Creston Middle School. At the time, I didn't want to leave all of my friends. But a few of my friends also got moved to Creston, and I made many new friends. Once middle school was over, I attended Warren Central High School for all four years.

IUPUI

Right after high school, I jumped right into college, at IUPUI like everybody recommends you do. Still being a kid, I decided to listen to everybody because I didn't know any better. My major was Fitness Management and Personal Training. I am very big into fitness so I thought this would be a good career. I soon realized that my passion for exercising did not translate over very well into my studies. After one long semester, I got an opportunity to work with an electrical company so I dropped out of IUPUI and started working full-time.

Ivy Tech

After 2 years of working full-time, I decided it was time to go back to school and get a degree. Coming into Ivy Tech, I was going for an AAS in Electrical Engineering. I had picked this major because I thought electricity was interesting. I soon found out that I was not as interested in math as I used to be in high school. So then I swtiched my major to Information Technology Support. I found this major to be very boring. Luckily, IT Support helped me find the Introduction to Software Development Class. While taking SDEV 140, I knew that this was the major I needed to pursue because it included everything I wanted in a major. That was when I discovered Website Development.
